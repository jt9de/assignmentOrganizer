Welcome to Assignment Organizer


With this application, our users can join classes created by professors. In these classes, students and professors can upload and download files associated with each class to aid a cooperative environment. Professors can upload events for their classes, includinsg an estimated time of completion, and students can see these assignments on each class page in list form, here on the homepage all together, or in the calendar in a clean calendar format. Students can even upload their own personal events to keep track of their own personal tasks.

We hope you enjoy using our assignment organizer!
